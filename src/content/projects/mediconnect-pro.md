## Transforming Patient Care

MediConnect Pro bridges the gap between digital convenience and quality healthcare.

### Challenges Solved

*   **Data Security:** Used private permissioned blockchain for immutable health records.
*   **Remote Consultation:** Integrated WebRTC for HD video calls even in low-bandwidth areas.
*   **AI Diagnostics:** Built a symptom checker with 92% accuracy compared to initial triage.

This project showcases our ability to handle sensitive data while delivering a consumer-grade user experience.
