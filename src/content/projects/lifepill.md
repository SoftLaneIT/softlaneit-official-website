LifePill provides a versatile solution for seamless medicine access and efficient prescription management. Developed between September 2023 and March 2024, it aims to streamline healthcare access.


### Key Features

- **Medicine Finder**: Easily locate pharmacies stocking required medications.
- **Pharmacy Integration**: Connects users directly with local and national pharmacy networks.
- **Digital Prescription Handling**: Securely upload, verify, and manage prescriptions across Web, Mobile, and Desktop platforms.
