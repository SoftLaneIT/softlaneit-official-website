## The Future of Retail

NeuralCommerce isn't just a shop; it's a personal shopping assistant.

### Innovation

We implemented a graph database (Neo4j) to map complex product relationships, allowing for "visual similarity" search that traditional text search engines miss. The frontend uses Three.js for 3D product previews directly in the browser.
