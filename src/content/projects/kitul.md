An elegant showcase and e-commerce platform for handcrafted Sri Lankan Kithul Treacle and Jaggery. This site combines cultural heritage with modern design, offering an immersive storytelling experience about the product's origins and traditional manufacturing processes.

### Key Highlights
- **Heritage Storytelling**: Engaging narrative sections detailing the traditional tapping and wood-fire boiling processes of Kithul making.
- **E-commerce Ready**: Features an intuitive product catalog and direct ordering channels optimized for local delivery.
- **Modern Aesthetic**: Clean, nature-inspired visual design that emphasizes organic authenticity and premium quality.
- **Fast Performance**: Built with modern web technologies for a smooth, app-like browsing experience.
- **Responsive Design**: Fully adaptable layout ensuring a perfect shopping experience on any device screen.
